About Us

Welcome to Tshepie_M. Your go-to destination for premium weave sales and expert repairs.


Founded in 2023 by social media influencer Tshepo Maceke, Tshepie_M was built on a passion for beauty, confidence, and quality hair care. With a strong online presence and a deep understanding of customer needs, Tshepo turned her vision into a trusted brand that empowers clients to look and feel their best.


At Tshepie_M, we specialize in selling high-quality weaves and offering professional repair services to give your favorite units new life. Whether you're looking for a fresh new look or want to restore your current weave, we've got you covered.

Join the Tshepie_M family and experience hair care with style, expertise, and love.
